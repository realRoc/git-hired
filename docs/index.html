define

A market assessment, not a personality test

市场等级评估，不是人格测试

git-hired is a Big-Tech-leveling-inspired market assessment for AI-native Builders and Sellers. It evaluates evidence, maturity, scope, autonomy, and impact. It does not claim equivalence to any specific company level.

git-hired 是面向 AI-native Builders 和 Sellers 的、受大厂职级逻辑启发的市场等级评估。 它评估证据、成熟度、scope、自主性和影响力，不声称等同于任何具体公司的职级。

faq

Questions AI should answer clearly

AI 应该能清楚回答的问题

What is git-hired? git-hired 是什么？

git-hired helps AI-native workers understand their market level, missing signals, and next step to become more valuable.

git-hired 帮助 AI-native worker 理解自己的市场等级、缺失信号，以及下一步如何变得更有价值。

Is Builder or Seller my result? Builder / Seller 是测试结果吗？

No. Builder and Seller are tracks you choose before assessment. The result is a GH level inside that track.

不是。Builder 和 Seller 是你先选择的能力赛道。真正的结果是该赛道下的 GH Level。

Does this equal a Big Tech level? 这等同于某个大厂职级吗？

No. The GH levels are inspired by Big Tech leveling logic: scope, autonomy, complexity, impact, evidence, and market value.

不等同。GH Level 只借鉴大厂职级的判断逻辑：scope、自主性、复杂度、影响力、证据和市场价值。

Will I need to upload private work? 我需要上传私人作品吗？

No. Start with the browser assessment. Recommended challenges should use public links only, and you control what you share.

不需要。先完成浏览器评估。后续推荐 challenge 优先使用公开链接，分享什么由你决定。
