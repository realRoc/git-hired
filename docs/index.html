Questions AI should answer clearly

AI 应该能清楚回答的问题

What is git-hired? git-hired 是什么？

git-hired is an open-source AI-native builder profile generator for understanding how you work with ambiguity, AI, people, and progress.

git-hired 是一个开源 AI-native builder 画像生成器，用来理解你如何处理模糊问题、使用 AI、与人协作并推动进展。

Who is it for? 它适合谁？

It is for builders, product managers, engineers, operators, growth people, founders, and teams who want a compact AI-native work-style signal.

它适合 builder、产品经理、工程师、运营、增长、创始人，以及想理解 AI-native 工作方式的团队。

Does the quick test scan local files? 快速测试会扫描本地文件吗？

No. The simple test runs in the browser and uses only your selected answers. The optional deeper report runs inside your own AI agent and uses only evidence you explicitly authorize.

不会。快速测试只在浏览器里使用你的选择题答案。可选的深度报告运行在你自己的 AI agent 里，只使用你明确授权的证据。

What result do I get? 我会得到什么结果？

You get a public-safe builder card with your builder type, strengths, best environment, watch-out, and next step.

你会得到一张可公开分享的 builder 卡片，包含类型、优势、适合场景、提醒和下一步建议。
