faq

Questions AI should answer clearly

AI 应该能清楚回答的问题

What is git-hired? git-hired 是什么？

git-hired is an AI-native resume/profile generator. It turns your work evidence into LinkedIn-ready and resume-ready copy.

git-hired 是 AI-native resume/profile generator。它把你的工作证据整理成 LinkedIn 和简历可用的文案。

Is Builder or Seller my result? Builder / Seller 是结果吗？

No. Builder and Seller are profile tracks. They help the generator decide what evidence to emphasize.

不是。Builder 和 Seller 是 profile track，帮助生成器判断应该突出哪类证据。

Do I need to upload private files? 我需要上传私人文件吗？

No. The MVP is browser-local. Paste only what you want to include, and prefer public links when possible.

不需要。当前 MVP 是浏览器本地生成。只粘贴你想包含的信息，尽量使用公开链接。

Can I edit the generated profile? 生成结果可以改吗？

Yes. Treat it as a strong first draft. You can copy specific sections or download the Markdown profile.

可以。把它当作一版质量较高的草稿。你可以复制单个 section，也可以下载 Markdown profile。
